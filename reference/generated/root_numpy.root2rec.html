3.4. root_numpy.root2rec¶

root2rec(filenames, treename=None, branches=None, selection=None, start=None, stop=None, step=None, include_weight=False, weight_name='weight')¶

View the result of root2array() as a record array.

See also

root2array

Notes

This is equivalent to:

root2array(filenames, treename, branches).view(np.recarray)
